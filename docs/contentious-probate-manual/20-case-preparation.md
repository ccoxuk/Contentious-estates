<!-- TODO: Expand strategic planning section -->

### Formulating the Case Theory

<!-- TODO: Add case theory development framework -->

1. Identify core legal issues
2. Map evidence to legal requirements
3. Identify gaps and weaknesses
4. Develop narrative
5. Plan how to prove each element

### Litigation vs. Settlement Analysis

<!-- TODO: Add decision tree for litigation strategy -->

**Factors Favoring Settlement:**
- Costs proportionality concerns
- Evidential weaknesses
- Client risk appetite
- Relationship preservation needs
- Certainty of outcome

**Factors Favoring Litigation:**
- Strong merits
- Principle at stake
- Opponent unreasonable
- No acceptable settlement offer
