### Initial Review Checklist (Within 7 Days of Opening)

<!-- TODO: Expand initial review requirements -->

## Peer Review Process

<!-- TODO: Add peer review procedures -->

### When Peer Review is Required

<!-- TODO: Detail peer review triggers -->

1. Complex or novel legal issues
2. High-value matters (over threshold)
3. Reputational risk cases
4. Conflicts of interest situations
5. Ethical dilemmas
6. Fee earner requests

### Peer Review Procedure

<!-- TODO: Add peer review process -->

1. **Request for Review**
   - Fee earner identifies need
   - Provides context and specific questions
   - Submits relevant documents

2. **Reviewer Selection**
   - Appropriate expertise
   - No conflict of interest
   - Sufficient seniority

3. **Review and Feedback**
   - Documented written feedback
   - Discussion if needed
   - Action points identified

4. **Implementation**
   - Fee earner implements feedback
   - Documents decisions made
   - Follow-up if necessary

## Risk Management

<!-- TODO: Add risk management framework -->

### Risk Register

<!-- TODO: Add risk register template and procedures -->

**Maintain risk register for each matter documenting:**
- Identified risks
- Risk rating (High/Medium/Low)
- Potential impact
- Mitigation measures
- Review date
- Responsible person

### Risk Escalation

<!-- TODO: Add risk escalation procedures -->

**Immediate escalation required for:**
- Missed limitation deadline
- Potential conflict of interest
- Breach of undertaking
- Possible professional negligence
- Client complaint
- Regulatory issue
- Significant adverse cost consequence

**Escalation Process:**
1. Immediate verbal notification to supervisor
2. Written incident report within 24 hours
3. Risk assessment and mitigation plan
4. Notification to professional indemnity insurers if appropriate
5. Documentation of all steps taken
