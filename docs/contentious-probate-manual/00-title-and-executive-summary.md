### Subtitle

## Executive Summary

This manual serves as a comprehensive resource for legal practitioners handling contentious probate matters. It provides:

- **Structured Guidance**: Step-by-step procedures for case preparation, drafting, and filing
- **Templates & Tools**: Ready-to-use templates for common documents and checklists
- **Best Practices**: Proven methodologies aligned with court requirements and professional standards
- **Quality Assurance**: Protocols for maintaining high standards in legal work product

### Scope

The manual covers:
- Initial case assessment and client instruction
- Pre-action protocols and correspondence
- Drafting pleadings and witness statements
- File management and administration
- Hearing and trial preparation
- Quality control and review processes

### Target Audience

- Solicitors handling contentious probate cases
- Legal executives and paralegals
- Support staff managing case files
- Trainee solicitors learning contentious probate practice

### Structure

The manual is organized into logical sections that follow the lifecycle of a contentious probate case, from initial instruction through to resolution. Each section includes practical guidance, templates, and quality assurance checkpoints.
