### Template: Letter Before Action - Contentious Probate

**File:** `template-letter-before-action-contentious-probate.md`

**Purpose:**
- Comply with pre-action protocol
- Set out legal case clearly
- Facilitate early resolution
- Preserve costs position

**When to Use:**
- Before issuing proceedings
- After exhausting informal approaches
- When formal action imminent

**Customization Guidance:**
- Specify cause of action precisely
- Include sufficient detail for informed response
- Propose ADR appropriately
- Set realistic response deadline (14-21 days typically)

**Critical Elements:**
- [ ] Clear statement of claim
- [ ] Factual basis summarized
- [ ] Legal grounds identified
- [ ] Relief sought specified
- [ ] Documents listed
- [ ] ADR proposed
- [ ] Response deadline stated
- [ ] Consequences of non-response outlined

## Court Document Templates

### Template: Witness Statement Skeleton

**File:** `template-witness-statement-skeleton.md`

**Purpose:**
- Provide structure for witness statements
- Ensure compliance with CPR Part 32
- Maintain consistency across statements

**When to Use:**
- Preparing factual witness evidence
- Trial or application evidence
- Disclosure statement

**Customization Guidance:**
- Complete in witness's own words
- Maintain first-person narrative
- Include only admissible evidence
- Exhibit documents correctly
- Number paragraphs consecutively

**Quality Checks:**
- [ ] Heading complete and accurate
- [ ] Introduction identifies witness
- [ ] Facts presented chronologically
- [ ] Exhibits properly marked
- [ ] Statement of truth included and signed
- [ ] Dates and facts verified

---

### Template: Particulars of Claim Skeleton

**File:** `template-particulars-of-claim-skeleton.md`

**Purpose:**
- Structure pleadings effectively
- Ensure all essential elements included
- Comply with CPR requirements

**When to Use:**
- Drafting claim
- Major amendments to claim

**Customization Guidance:**
- Adapt to specific cause of action
- Include material facts only
- Specify relief sought precisely
- Avoid unnecessary detail or evidence

**Common Variations:**
- Lack of testamentary capacity claim
- Undue influence claim
- Want of knowledge and approval
- Inheritance Act 1975 claim
- Proprietary estoppel claim

**Drafting Principles:**
- Concise and clear
- Numbered paragraphs
- Chronological narrative
- Material facts, not evidence
- Verified by statement of truth

---

### Template: Consent Order or Tomlin Order Heads

**File:** `template-consent-order-or-tomlin-order-heads.md`

**Purpose:**
- Record settlement terms
- Create enforceable agreement
- Resolve litigation efficiently

**When to Use:**
- Settlement reached
- Compromise agreed
- Mediation successful

**Types:**

**Simple Consent Order:**
- Settlement terms in body of order
- Public record
- Directly enforceable

**Tomlin Order:**
- Stay of proceedings
- Terms in confidential schedule
- Privacy maintained
- Enforceable by application to lift stay

**Customization Guidance:**
- Ensure all terms clear and certain
- Address costs comprehensively
- Include enforcement provisions
- Consider tax implications
- Obtain client approval before finalizing

## Using Templates Effectively

### General Principles

**1. Understand Before Using:**
- Read template fully before completing
- Understand purpose and context
- Review relevant manual section

**2. Customize Appropriately:**
- Every case is different
- Adapt template to specific circumstances
- Delete irrelevant sections
- Add case-specific content

**3. Maintain Quality:**
- Templates are starting point, not finished product
- Apply professional judgment
- Review and refine
- Peer review for critical documents

**4. Keep Current:**
- Check for latest version
- Note any updates or amendments
- Provide feedback for improvements

### Version Control for Templates

**Template Versions:**
- Templates versioned separately from case documents
- Check master template library for latest version
- Note template version used in case file

**Updates:**
- Templates updated periodically
- Review change log for significant updates
- Retrofit important updates to active cases
