## Introduction

<!-- TODO: Add overview of template library purpose and usage -->

This section provides an overview of the templates, checklists, and appendices available to support contentious probate practice. These resources are designed to improve efficiency, consistency, and quality across all contentious probate matters.

## How to Use the Templates

<!-- TODO: Add detailed usage instructions -->

### General Guidelines

1. **Adaptation Required**
   - All templates require customization for specific cases
   - Review and modify all placeholder text
   - Ensure factual accuracy
   - Verify legal citations are current

2. **Version Control**
   - Save your customized version with appropriate filename
   - Maintain template originals unchanged
   - Track modifications to templates

3. **Quality Control**
   - Always have supervisor review before use
   - Cross-check against current law and practice
   - Ensure compliance with court requirements

### Template Categories

<!-- TODO: Add category descriptions -->

- **Correspondence Templates:** Client and third-party letters
- **Court Document Templates:** Pleadings and applications
- **Settlement Templates:** Consent orders and settlement terms
- **Checklists:** Procedural and quality assurance checklists
- **Administrative Templates:** File management and case tracking
